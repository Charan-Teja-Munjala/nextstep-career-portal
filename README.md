NextStep Hub is a comprehensive, dual-portal web application designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and professional employment. It serves as a unified ecosystem where students can upskill and find jobs, while organizations can provide educational content and recruit talent.

For Students

Professional Resume Builder: Automatically generate a polished, printable resume based on your profile data.

Verifiable Certifications: Earn unique certificates upon 100% course completion, featuring QR codes for instant employer verification.

Personal Dashboard: A central hub to track enrolled courses, application status, and professional growth.

For Organizations
CMS for Courses: Create and manage multi-module courses with support for video, text, and document-based lessons.

Recruitment Tools: Post detailed job listings and manage the entire applicant pipeline from a single dashboard.

Analytics & Insights: Monitor student enrollment numbers and job application counts in real-time.

Talent Pipeline: Directly access a pool of motivated students who are actively learning industry-relevant skills.
